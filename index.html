Rome, Italy – A Dream Realized

My unforgettable journey to the heart of history

About My Trip

In October 2023, I visited Rome just before embarking on a cruise. We spent four unforgettable days exploring the city's breathtaking history, architecture, and culture. Staying in a beautiful hotel and walking the timeless streets was a dream come true. I had longed to visit since elementary school, and I finally made it just months after graduating high school.

Top Sights

  • Colosseum: The grandeur and legacy of ancient gladiators.
  • St. Peter’s Basilica: Awe-inspiring architecture and sacred art.
  • Spanish Steps: A bustling and scenic Roman landmark.
  • Museums: Featuring ancient marble sculptures, masterful paintings, and centuries-old artifacts.

Why You Should Visit

Rome is one of the most breathtaking cities in the world. It’s walkable, steeped in history, and home to the most delicious, authentic Italian cuisine. From every street corner to every stone path, history surrounds you. It’s a once-in-a-lifetime experience that everyone should embrace at least once.

Tips for Future Travelers

  • Visit in October for pleasant weather and lighter crowds.
  • Wear comfortable shoes – you'll be doing a lot of walking.
  • Don’t miss out on local dishes like carbonara and freshly made gelato.

A Note from Me

Rome changed my life. I would give anything to go back and stay even longer. If you ever have the opportunity to go—don’t hesitate. Let the Eternal City inspire you the way it inspired me.
